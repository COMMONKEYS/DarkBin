What is Rarbin?

Rarbin is a document sharing and publishing website for text-based information. There are no limitations on what info you can post as long as it's not spam, child explicit material or violating hosting country jurisdictional laws. For example, kiddie porn links, your mothers creditcard and any support of terrorism/threats of physical violence is not allowed here.

Rarbin is not your personal army, nor your personal slam page.

User information:

The only information we keep is the information you give us upon registration (email address & hashed password).

IP addresses are not retained in the access logs (In fact, even if someone tcpdumped everything, it would just show a bunch of traffic coming from 127.0.0.1)

Content Guidelines:

Rarbin provides a platform for users to upload text information without the fear of censorship. All content hosted on Rarbin is subject to removal under certain
conditions, including but not limited to, violations of this policy or the issuance of a court order in accordance with the applicable laws where Rarbin operates
Rarbin reserves the right, at its sole discretion, to remove any content from the platform for any reason, including but not limited to violations of this policy.

The following content is prohibited on our platform:
- Personal information of individuals under the age of 15
- Links to explicit material involving minors
- Dox requests or any form of targeted harassment
- Spam or malicious content
- Direct threats of physical harm, terrorism, or swatting
- Reposting identical or low quality content

To report violations of this policy, please contact us via Telegram at @hologramic.
Law enforcement inquiries must be directed to legal@rarbin.com in accordance with our Law Enforcement Request Guidelines. All emails received from anyone other than authorized law enforcement officials or attorneys representing law enforcement agencies will be directed to our Telegram support.

Legal:

Rarbin and its administrators adhere to all applicable laws within the jurisdictions in which the platform operates. Rarbin assumes no responsibility for the content uploaded by its users.

- Rarbin cannot be held liable for any content uploaded by users in adherence to Section 230 of the Communications Act of 1934, which provides immunity from liability for user-generated content published by third parties.
- The platform does not endorse, participate in, or facilitate any form of harassment, cyberbullying, or threats of violence.
- Any content that directly threatens, incites harm, or is in violation of applicable law will be removed immediately upon review.
